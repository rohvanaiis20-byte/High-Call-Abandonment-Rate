AI Voice Assistant to Reduce High Call Abandonment Rate in Call Centres

A smart AI-powered voice assistant that answers customer calls instantly, reduces waiting time, automates FAQs, routes calls intelligently, and improves customer satisfaction.

Explore Project

Project Introduction

Many organizations experience a high call abandonment rate because customers wait too long before speaking with an agent. This AI Voice Assistant provides immediate assistance, resolves common enquiries, and transfers only complex cases to live agents.

Business Problem

High Call Abandonment

Customers hang up before reaching customer service due to long waiting times.

Long Waiting Queue

Peak-hour traffic creates long queues and delays.

Limited Agents

Insufficient staff during busy hours increases customer frustration.

Common Causes

  • Long waiting queues
  • Insufficient customer service agents
  • Slow call routing
  • Repetitive enquiries
  • Customers become frustrated while waiting

Business Impact

  • Loss of customer confidence
  • Lower customer satisfaction (CSAT)
  • Higher operational costs
  • Negative brand reputation
  • Lost business opportunities

Project Objectives

Reduce Waiting Time

Answer customer calls immediately.

Improve Customer Satisfaction

Provide faster and more efficient support.

Improve Service Efficiency

Allow live agents to focus on complex issues.

Smart AI Voice Assistant

The AI Voice Assistant acts as the first point of contact. Customers receive instant assistance without waiting in a queue. The AI can:

  • Answer FAQs instantly
  • Collect customer information
  • Route calls intelligently
  • Transfer difficult cases to live agents
  • Provide 24/7 customer service

AI Features

Instant Voice Response

AI answers incoming calls immediately.

Smart Call Routing

Routes customers to the correct department.

FAQ Assistant

Answers billing, account, payment and internet enquiries.

Complaint Collection

Collects customer details before transferring to an agent.

24/7 Service

Available anytime, even outside business hours.

Sample Conversation

AI: Hello! Welcome to TNB Healthcare Customer Service. How may I assist you today?

Customer: I want to know how many times I can use a referral letter for Diabetes Mellitus.

AI: Sure. May I know your full name and IC number?

Customer: I'm Sara. My IC number is 970913-08-6666.

AI: Kindly hold while I verify your information.

AI: Thank you for waiting. A referral letter for Diabetes Mellitus can be used two times.

Customer: Thank you.

AI: You're welcome. Have a wonderful day!
